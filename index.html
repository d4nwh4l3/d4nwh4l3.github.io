About Me

I am a web designer and Quality Assurance specialist with a strong foundation in building intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. I recently graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with a degree in Digital Narrative and Interactive Design, alongside minors in Computer Science, Information Science, and Creative Writing. My experience spans both public and private sectors. As a Web Design Intern for Allegheny County, I contributed to the design and maintenance of public-facing websites, improving usability and accessibility for a wide audience. Most recently, I served as a Software Quality Assurance Intern at Imagine360, where I worked across multiple projects, including IVR and Rx Claims, developing test strategies, validating data pipelines, and ensuring the reliability of new system integrations. Beyond internships, I’ve led and collaborated on projects such as WandAR, a mapping app designed for students. These projects highlight my ability to blend storytelling, design, and technology into practical, innovative solutions. While my expertise is strongest in front-end development, I’m also skilled with back-end tools and languages, giving me the flexibility to work across the full stack. I’m continually expanding my knowledge and love exploring new technologies. Outside of work, I enjoy experimenting with interactive media projects, practicing saxophone, gaming, and reading.
